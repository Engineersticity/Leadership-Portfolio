Studies and Work Experience
Schools and Mentors

If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of Giants. These Giants are my Parents, Teachers and Mentors. I owe all my success to their sacrifice, encouragements, support and the belief they have had and still have on me.

Overview

Primary School

Kimogoro TF Junior Academy (TF)

This is where my education began. I still remember how we hated waking up at 5 am for the 'Morning Pre'. The Most memorable thing from my Primary school is a quote I heard from our Headmaster, "The height the great men attaained and maintained, was not achieved in a single flight, but they struggled for when the rest were asleep." It taught me the importance of hardwork.

High School

The Alliance High School

Alliance High school is the best Public High School in Kenya. It was a privilidge getting an admission to Alliance. I was exposed and my mind opened because the community was diverse. It was also here that my leadership journey began. I headed the Electronics Club and became the school Volleyball Captain. It was at Alliance that I gre up tobecome a man. I joined as a regular village boy and left as a grown man with a vision.The school Principal at the time Mr. David Gideon Kariuki is still one of my most admired leader and mentor. The whole Alliance community changed my life for the better.

University

Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT)

They say that JKUAT is the best University in Kenya to study Engineering. I joined JKUAT to study Telecommunications and Information Engineering. It was the most prestigious course to do at the moment and I had adequately passed my High school to enroll to do it. It was at JKUAT that I started building my network and making connections that will later land me to the best University in Africa, The African Leadership University (ALU)

University

The African Leadership University (ALU)

ALU is pioneering a fresh approach to higher education in the 21st century; offering accredited undergraduate, postgraduate and executive education programmes in a unique and imaginative way. By integrating students’ learning with the real world, empowering students to take ownership of their own learning, equipping each student to think entrepreneurially, and employing the most engaging and inspiring teaching methods, ALU is pioneering a new take on higher education and leadership development. ALU is the University of the 21st Century, the modern University. What I love about ALU is the community. We are student from 27 different countries at ALU Rwanda. This means 27 different languages, cultures and mindsets. There is no day in ALU that I don't learn at ALU.

My Mentors

The Giants on whose Shoulders I have stood

David Ngetich

My Dad

Besides being a father and a provider to me, He has been a mentor and a source of inspiration. The ultimate thing that he has done to to me is belief in me. When I decided to leave my previous University to join ALU after 3 years, he had his doubts but he trusted my decision and allowed me to chase my dreams. He is a giant, and I will forever stand on his shoulders.

Florence Ngetich

My Mum

A mom is a mom. My mothers love is what has always pushed me to pursue excellence. During my time in High school, she would always tell me to remember to work hard in school. Her voice and intonation at that moment always pushed me to read and study hard. She has made our family happier and happier and home is never home without mom. She is a Giant, a great one, and I would never who I want to be if I get off your shoulders.

David Gideon Kariuki

Former Alliance High School Principal

I joined Alliance High School as naive village boy. Mr. Kariuki made it possible for me to thrive in a community that was more exposed than I was. He gave me the confidence that still drives me today. The Most memorable quote from Kariuki is, "If it is to be, it is up to me" He called it the best combination of 2-word words. He is a Giant whose shoulders takes me to the roots of Excellence and Persistence.

Fred Swaniker

ALU Founder

I first met Fred in 2012. He came to our school to speak to us on the speech day. Listening to him speak made me realize me realize and start to think about ewhat I wanted to do with my life. He talked about how Africa was taking a turn for the worse and that it was time that we stand and defend the continent. He was already doing this through the African Leadership Academy. Later on when Fred started the African Leaddership University with a vision to produce 3 million leaders by 2060, I decided that I was gonna be one of the leaders that will change Africa. Fred is a Giant whose shoulders are giving me access to world class education and an opportunity to change Africa.

Hamed Kourouma

Founder Savi-Labs and POSIS Technologies

Currently I am the Head of Spin-offs, Tech transfer and Entrepreneurship at Savi-Labs. Savi-Labs is an organisation that is promoting STEM disciplines in Africa. I met Hamed when he approached me to become the first Head of Spin-offs, Tech transfer and Entrepreneurship for his newly formed organisation, Savi-Labs. Since then, My life has not been the same. He has showed me the real power of networking and has even helped me get an internship with SuperFluid Labs, a consulting company that uses AI to analize trends and advice their clients on the next course of action regarding their ventures and businesses.
